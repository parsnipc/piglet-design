Project brief

Improve the onboarding experience for the parents of new users of our literacy and numeracy software for children.

My role

  • UX design
  • Manage front end implementation

Tools used

  • Google analytics
  • Inspectlet - screen capture
  • Balsamiq
  • InVision
  • PowerPoint
  • HTML/CSS (Sublime + CodePen + Bootstrap)
  • Photoshop

Defining the problem

My first step was to define the problem I was trying to solve. I looked at existing data and filled in any gaps with extra research. I had findings from user interviews conducted by a customer engagement agency and added to that some quantitive analytics data around drop off points in the user onboarding funnel and the results of recent customer survey. I also spoke to our customer service team to understand any key pain points experienced by our users and prepared onboarding teardowns of our product and competitor products for reference.
