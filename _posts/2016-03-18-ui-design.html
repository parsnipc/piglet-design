Client

Mathseeds, a numeracy program for children aged 3 to 8 was launching to schools in the US and School District Coordinators needed a way to manage the new maths program and existing Reading Eggs program.

The brief

Create a transactional interface for US schools district coordinators to manage subscriptions and track school engagement and show student progress with the Reading and Maths software.

I worked with our business analyst, product manager and development team to bring the design from intial brief to implementation.

My role

  • Lead Designer
  • Manage Front End Implementation
