Dept. of Education Brand Identity

The CoSiES (Collaboration of Schools in the Eastern Suburbs) program is a Dept of Education and Communities initiative to promote collaboration between local schools on promotional and educational activities.

The CoSiES Program needed branding and marketing materials highlighting the values of the program including education, shared ideas and the coastal location of the participating schools.
